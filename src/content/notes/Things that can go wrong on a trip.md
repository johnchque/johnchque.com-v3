title: Things that can go wrong on a trip
date: 2026-01-21
updated: 2026-01-21
description: A list of things
category: Reflections
---
- Passport not having the minimum required validation time left for a country you are just passing by.
- Luggage being lost and having to travel the next day.
- Customs not believing your documents are authentic.
- Losing your passport/ID or so and having to fly the next day.
- Sleeping over the meals and waking up too late to get them.
- Not being able to get seats together with whom you are travelling with.
- Spending too much time in passport control so you lose your taxi.
- The hotel does not look as in the photos.
- Getting food poisoning multiple days before going back home.
